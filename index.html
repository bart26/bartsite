About

Mao-Cheng Lee received the B.S and M.S degree from National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2011 and 2013, respectively. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering and computer science at the University of California, Irvine, CA, USA. His research interests include mixed-signal and low power integrated circuit design for brain-computer interface (BCI) applications. From 2013 to 2015 he was with Airoha Technology Corp. (Mediatek Subsidiary), Taiwan, where he was involved in the design of frequency synthesizers and calibration control systems for Wi-Fi, satellite TV tuner and Bluetooth products.
